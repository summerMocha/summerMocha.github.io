Headline analysis using TextBlob and NLTK:

An exploration 10,000 news articles collected between November 2016 and May 2017 from the top-500 news publishers. Data analysis always begins with questions.I was working with a dataset of 10,000 stories from the top-500 news publishers in the U.S. Before I could determine what to filter out for analysis, I had to define my questions.

  • Sentiment Analysis among Headline
  • Which Publisher sent out the most articles
  • What are the top keywords
  • Proportion of Positive VS Negative news
  • What day of week are likely to be towed
