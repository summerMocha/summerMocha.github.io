The Gilded Fest: A combination of Music and Technology

oct,1 2016 by Wei Tang

It was a cold, rainy day but that didn’t keep people away from The Gilded Fest that took place in Boston.

The Gilded Fest is a festival that combines technology and innovation with music. There was a concert and booths of emerging technology that people could try.

Concert combining the newest and hottest technology and innovation with music. The people pictured are volunteers who help people get involved. Volunteers are enjoying the swings while they wait for people to arrive.
